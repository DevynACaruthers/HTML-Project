The Beginning

Sounbreaker studio started with the creator of the company: Devyn Caruthers. It was his vision to pioneer a world where people aren't anchored down by constrictive contracts put in place to limit the creative freedom of animators, scriptwriters, and developers. He wanted to help make his dream a reality by creating a safe haven for creators to make whatever their heart desires, along with not being hindered by what was popular at the time or follow trends but create something truly timeless. His first game was a snake game he may or may not have copied the majority of from his coding teacher, designed for a competition for one of his clubs. Unfortunately, it failed and acted more as a lesson then it's original purpose of being fun.

Future Projects

The first game planning to be made by the studio is a parody of CS:GO currently without a title, taking place in an office setting where people use typical office supplies as weapons while planting a calculator "bomb" at the targetted location. However, it would not be that simple since we were also inspired by games like "Valorant" and "Rainbow Six: Siege" by adding characters with unique abilites! This would drastically change combat and make it more strategic and engaging to the general audience, not to mention let the team have fun with designing a bunch of different characters, (It won't be). We have others, but the ideas will have to develop more so stay tuned!
